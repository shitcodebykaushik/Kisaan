FAQs

First enter your number, and OTP on the home page, If not signed up on our database, it will not show the Farmer's Portal. It will redirect to a sign up page, where you can select your type, add your details.

Once details are added, it will redirect you to the farmer or wholesaler's portal depending on what you chose in sign up page.

For adding items, there will be a plus icon under category, in manage items, which will redirect you to an Add Items page. You can add the category, item and fill in all the details, and press add.

The item will be added to your list on Farmer's Portal.

For removing items from current orders, and move to delivered orders, you have to click on tick button on the extreme right corner of the list.
Your item will be moved to past delivered items.
